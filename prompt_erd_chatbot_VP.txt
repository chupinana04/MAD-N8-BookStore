Draw an Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) for the Chatbot (Virtual Assistant) database schema.

Entities and Attributes:

1. Entity "Book"
   - bookID: integer (Primary Key)
   - title: varchar
   - price: decimal
   - description: text
   - width: decimal
   - height: decimal
   - thickness: decimal
   - weight: integer
   - totalPage: integer
   - format: varchar

2. Entity "Authors"
   - authorID: integer (Primary Key)
   - authorName: varchar
   - biography: text

3. Entity "BookAuthor"
   - idBook: integer (Foreign Key)
   - idAuthor: integer (Foreign Key)

Relationships:

1. One-to-Many relationship from "Book" to "BookAuthor". Foreign key: idBook
2. One-to-Many relationship from "Authors" to "BookAuthor". Foreign key: idAuthor
