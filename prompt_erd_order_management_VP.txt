Draw an Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) for the Order Management database schema.

Entities and Attributes:

1. Entity "Order"
   - orderID: integer (Primary Key)
   - orderDate: timestamp
   - totalAmount: decimal
   - finalAmount: decimal
   - status: varchar
   - vnpTransactionNo: varchar
   - idCustomer: integer (Foreign Key)
   - idAddress: integer (Foreign Key)
   - idPayment: integer (Foreign Key)
   - idShipment: integer (Foreign Key)
   - idVoucher: integer (Foreign Key)

2. Entity "OrderItem"
   - orderItemID: integer (Primary Key)
   - quantity: integer
   - idOrder: integer (Foreign Key)
   - idBook: integer (Foreign Key)

3. Entity "Customer"
   - customerID: integer (Primary Key)
   - fullName: varchar

4. Entity "Book"
   - bookID: integer (Primary Key)
   - title: varchar
   - price: decimal

5. Entity "Address"
   - addressID: integer (Primary Key)
   - receiverName: varchar
   - addressString: varchar

6. Entity "Payment"
   - paymentID: integer (Primary Key)
   - paymentMethod: varchar
   - status: varchar
   - vnpToken: varchar

7. Entity "Shipment"
   - shipmentID: integer (Primary Key)
   - shipmentMethod: varchar
   - estimatedDate: varchar

8. Entity "Voucher"
   - voucherID: integer (Primary Key)
   - code: varchar
   - type: varchar
   - discountValue: decimal
   - usageLimit: integer

Relationships:

1. One-to-Many relationship from "Customer" to "Order". Foreign key: idCustomer
2. One-to-Many relationship from "Address" to "Order". Foreign key: idAddress
3. One-to-Many relationship from "Payment" to "Order". Foreign key: idPayment
4. One-to-Many relationship from "Shipment" to "Order". Foreign key: idShipment
5. One-to-Many relationship from "Voucher" to "Order". Foreign key: idVoucher
6. One-to-Many relationship from "Order" to "OrderItem". Foreign key: idOrder
7. One-to-Many relationship from "Book" to "OrderItem". Foreign key: idBook
