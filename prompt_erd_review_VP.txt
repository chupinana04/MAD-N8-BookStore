Draw an Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) for the Product Review database schema.

Entities and Attributes:

1. Entity "Customer"
   - customerID: integer (Primary Key)
   - fullName: varchar
   - email: varchar
   - phoneNumber: varchar

2. Entity "Book"
   - bookID: integer (Primary Key)
   - title: varchar
   - price: decimal
   - author: varchar

3. Entity "Review"
   - reviewID: integer (Primary Key)
   - rating: integer
   - comment: text
   - idCustomer: integer (Foreign Key)
   - idBook: integer (Foreign Key)
   - createdAt: timestamp

Relationships:

1. One-to-Many relationship from "Customer" to "Review". (One Customer can write zero or many Reviews). The foreign key is idCustomer.
2. One-to-Many relationship from "Book" to "Review". (One Book can have zero or many Reviews). The foreign key is idBook.
